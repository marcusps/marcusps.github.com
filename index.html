Marcus P. da Silva

I am a physicist, having studied under Prof. Raymond Laflamme and Prof. Frank Wilhelm at the Institute for Quantum Computing (University of Waterloo, Canada). I am now a member of the Quantum Information Processing group at Raytheon BBN Technologies (Cambridge, MA), and I am interested in various topics related to quantum information processing, ranging from practical protocols for the verification of quantum devices, to proposals for physical implementations of quantum devices.
